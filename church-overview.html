CHURCH OVERVIEW

WHAT IS PRAYER COVER FOR CHURCHES?

Prayer Cover helps churches create a simple and sustainable rhythm of prayer for the communities they serve. Members participate by praying regularly for specific streets within the church's city, creating a growing network of faithful prayer throughout the community.

PARTNERSHIP WITH YOUR CHURCH

Prayer Cover provides the technology, mapping, maintenance, support, and ongoing development needed to keep the system running smoothly. From coding updates and technical support to email changes and street management, we handle the platform so your church can focus on ministry.

CHURCH PARTICIPATION

Each participating church receives a unique Church Campus Code. Churches determine the level of participation that best reflects their anticipated involvement. Church participation can grow over time as interest grows within the congregation.

INVITING MEMBERS

Prayer Cover provides churches with videos, invitation materials, QR codes, and member resources designed to help introduce Prayer Cover to the congregation.

Initial launch materials are provided to help churches begin participation, and updated resources are provided periodically to encourage ongoing involvement and engagement.

MEMBERS-ONLY PARTICIPATION

We encourage churches to treat Church Campus Codes as member resources. Codes do not need to be publicly displayed during services, livestreams, or public broadcasts. This helps churches steward participation while keeping access aligned with the congregation they serve.

WEEKLY COMMUNITY PRAYER

The Prayer Cover map is live and can be refreshed at any time to view participation and street coverage in real time. As Streetkeepers claim and cover streets throughout the week, their involvement is reflected on the map.

At the beginning of each week, the map refreshes to begin a new cycle of prayer, creating an ongoing rhythm of participation rather than a one-time event.

CHURCH COOPERATION

The Prayer Cover map reflects prayer activity throughout a community, but it does not identify individual Streetkeepers or churches on the map itself.

This decision was intentional.

Prayer Cover was designed to encourage cooperation rather than competition. Every Streetkeeper is represented in the same way, and every church's participation is reflected in the same way.

The focus remains on prayer coverage throughout the community rather than on comparing churches, measuring performance, or highlighting individual involvement.

As churches participate together, the map becomes a shared picture of prayer taking place throughout a city. Our hope is that churches will see themselves as partners in a larger effort rather than separate groups working independently of one another.

The goal is not recognition.

The goal is prayer.

The goal is that homes, businesses, schools, neighborhoods, and communities would be faithfully covered in prayer by God's people working together.

GETTING STARTED

Getting started is simple. Prayer Cover will provide the tools, resources, and support needed to help your church begin participating and inviting members into a consistent rhythm of community prayer.
